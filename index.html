This analysis identifies key drivers of customer churn and develops a high-performance XGBoost model to predict it. The primary risk factors are Month-to-Month contracts and low tenure. Actionable recommendations focus on incentivizing long-term contracts and improving early customer engagement to mitigate churn.

2. Exploratory Data Analysis

Overall Churn Distribution

The dataset is imbalanced, with churners representing about a quarter of the customer base.

Churn by Contract Type

Month-to-month contracts are the single largest predictor of churn, indicating a lack of long-term commitment.

4. Actionable Recommendations

  1. Incentivize Long-Term Contracts: Proactively target month-to-month customers with offers to upgrade. High Impact
  2. Implement Early-Tenure Engagement Program: Create a dedicated onboarding journey for customers in their first 3 months. Medium Impact
  3. Optimize Fiber Optic Value Proposition: Ensure the value is clear for this high-cost, high-churn service through marketing or bundling. Medium Impact

Appendix

Technical Details

  • Python Version: 3.9.12
  • Library Versions: scikit-learn (1.1.1), xgboost (1.6.1), pandas (1.4.2), seaborn (0.11.2)
  • Train/Test Split: 80% training, 20% testing (test_size=0.2).
